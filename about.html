About Us

UX/UI Explorers is a student-ran club all about design, user

experience, and user interface. Based at Orange Coast College,

we provide a space for any creative person to join us in events,

workshops, and projects. UX/UI Explorers Club is relatively new,

as it was founded in fall 2021. The people behind it are ambitious

people, and we hope you'll be there to see it. We aim to be creative

and encourage collabortation as we go through learning the user

experience process together! Any skill level is welcomed.
